Hey there!

Welcome to Bauinfrastruktur, your reliable partner in the field of civil engineering and infrastructure design. We are pleased to present a comprehensive offer of specialised services, designed to ensure feasibility and excellence in the execution of your projects.

Infrastructures

we offer comprehensive consultancy services in geotechnics, structures, hydraulics and hydrology for civil infrastructure projects. From the evaluation of soils to the design of drainage systems, we offer the different service in the evaluation and stability of works such as bridges, buildings, pipelines, roads through technical solutions adapted to each project, based on the legal regulations in force.

Energy

We provide consulting engineering services in the design of renewable energy projects such as mini-hydro, solar, and wind farms. From renewable resource assessment to distribution network design, we help our clients implement sustainable and efficient solutions for their energy needs.

Mining

For the mining industry, we offer geotechnical, environmental and occupational health and safety consulting services. From geotechnical risk assessment to environmental management and implementation of occupational safety programs, we are committed to ensuring sustainability and safety at all stages of mining operations.

    Hydraulics and Hydrology

    We offer effective solutions to manage water resources based on the knowledge and experience of our technical team. From irrigation systems to watershed management, we address water challenges in projects of any size, ensuring efficiency and sustainability.

    Geotechnical designs

    We have expertise in geotechnical design, ensuring the stability and safety of structures. Our experience ranges from ground evaluation to the selection of foundation systems and slope stability, guaranteeing solid and durable solutions for each project.

    Building Information Modeling

    We implement BIM technology to optimize projects. Our 3D approach improves interdisciplinary coordination, increasing efficiency and accuracy. We use up-to-date data for informed decisions and effective collaboration between all project parties.

    Geometric design

    We develop geometric designs in compliance with national standards, optimizing functionality and safety in road and rail infrastructures. Our attention is focused on construction details, offering solutions aligned with the interests of our clients.

    Topographic and Geodetic

    We provide accurate surveying services using advanced technology, such as RTK and total stations, ensuring reliable and detailed data. In addition, we employ drones and satellite technology for comprehensive analysis and precise controls at all stages of the project.

    Structural design

    Structural designs governed by current seismic-resistant regulations. We prioritize safety and stability in all our structures by complying with the different safety factors, ensuring that each project meets the highest standards of quality and resilience to structural requests.
