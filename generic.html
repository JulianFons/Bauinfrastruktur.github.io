PROFESIONAL EXPERIENCE


Julian Fonseca, M.Sc.

Master’s degree in Infrastructure Planning from the University of Stuttgart, Germany, and a Civil Engineer with eight years of professional experience in the analysis, design, and execution of civil projects such as dams, wind and solar farms, tunnels, and buildings. Julian has forged knowledge in pre-feasibility, feasibility, construction, operation, and rehabilitation, always maintaining economical, technical, social, and environmental cohesion over the years of work experience in projects such as the Ituango hydroelectric plant, with an installed capacity of 2400 MW.

Carlos Borrero, Master BIM Management.

Master BIM Manager in Civil Infrastructure and Surveyor Engineer of the Technological Units of Santander, with eight years of experience in the construction of civil and mechanical works, in calculation and stakeout of earthworks, metal structures, etc. For five years contributing and acquiring knowledge in the mining area participating in projects such as "Mina De Oro Buriticá - Antioquia", "Gold Mine Marmato - Caldas", as a 3D modeler of containment works, road design, tailings deposit, layout of electrical networks, design of tunnels and site controls, being civil 3D the main tool for the development of the activities.
